About Us

Project Description

Project Lodi is designed to help students with one of their daily dilemmas in school: where to eat for breaks? The name was inspired by a fad in the Philippines in the form of a Facebook group named “What’s your ulam pare” where in members upload photos of their meal(s) for the day.
